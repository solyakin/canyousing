The competition, seeks for raw voice music talent, and will be testing from voice to song arrangement, stage management, audience retention, writing skills. How to take part?

  • Register by clicking on 'Audition Form' and pay the registration fee of N2000
  • Note down your order ID
  • Upload a video of yourself singing or rapping using a white background. Pick any song of your choice and sing WITHOUT A SOUNDTRACK. We want to hear your raw voice. {VIDEO MUST NOT BE LONGER THAN 2 MINS}

  • You need to use this link to get likes on the videos and these likes on your video will carry you to get into the next round of the competition.
